Privacy Policy

The short version

We rely on third-party services, which collect and retain IP addresses. Except for IP addresses, we do not collect, store, or process any potentially personally identifiable information.

Data collected by third parties

We rely on the following third-party services to provide this website: Firebase Hosting, Cloud Firestore.

Firebase Hosting collects IP addresses to detect abuse and provide us with detailed analysis of usage data. IP addresses are retained by Firebase Hosting for a few months and are never accessible to us.

Data collected directly by us

We do not directly collect, store, or process any personally identifiable information.

To measure the impact of our services, we collect the following information:

  • When a page is loaded on this website, we store:
    • The time the page was loaded (with nanosecond precision)
    • Which page was loaded
  • When a link or button is clicked on this website, we store:
    • The time the button was clicked (with nanosecond precision)
    • Which button was clicked
  • When a QR code on one of our posters is scanned to access this website, we store:
    • The date (in UTC) the QR code was scanned (e.g. January 1st, 1970)
    • The unique identifier of the QR code
      • This unique identifier is associated with the geographic coordinates where the poster was placed
